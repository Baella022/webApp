Welcome to Grade 6 Science!

Get ready for an exciting journey of discovery in the world of science. This year, we will explore fascinating topics like energy, electricity, forces, Earth's gravity, the moon, stars, plants, animals, and the human body.

Through hands-on experiments and interactive learning, we will deepen our understanding of the natural phenomena that surround us. Let's embrace curiosity, question the world around us, and develop critical thinking skills. Get ready to unlock the secrets of science!

Wishing you an amazing year filled with exploration, wonder, and scientific breakthroughs!
